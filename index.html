Welcome to my website!

My name is Katie Boss, and I'm a current History MA candidate, Public History Certificate student, and Teaching Assistant at UMass Amherst. I am invested in advocating for historical thinking, awareness, and appreciation for my students and the public, as well as promoting archives, museums, and historical societies in a variety of functions. My research generally focuses on the United States after ~1865, although I'm particularly interested in the history of psychiatry, disability, journalism, film/TV, and New England.

I'm originally from the rural town of Exeter, Rhode Island, and attended the University of Rhode Island for my undergraduate degrees in History and Art History. During my time there, I gained valuable experience working in archives, museums, and art galleries. I can often be found at independent cinemas, used book stores, concerts, or traveling throughout New England. In my free time, I enjoy drawing, listening to my CDs and tapes, playing video games, and watching movies.
